Instructions

Concentration, also known as Match Match or simply Pairs, is a card game in which all of the cards are laid face down on a surface and two cards are flipped face up over each turn. The object of the game is to turn over pairs of matching cards.

In this special version Concentration called the Memoria Game you will have to use your knowledge of Italian vocabulary to match each card to its respective translations!
